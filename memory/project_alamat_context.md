ALAMAT stands for **Assisted Learning for Mastery and Assessment Training**. It is a Philippine exam reviewer app targeting UPCAT, other college entrance exams (ACET, DCAT, USTET), and Civil Service Exams (Professional and Sub-Professional levels).

**Why:** The name and branding should reflect intelligent, adaptive learning — not just flashcard drilling.

**How to apply:** Feature suggestions and copy should lean into the "assisted/adaptive" angle — personalized feedback, weakness detection, progress-driven recommendations.
